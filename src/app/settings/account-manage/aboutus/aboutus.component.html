About Us

Welcome to AniStream, your go-to platform for streaming the latest and greatest in anime and manga! This site was built with passion by Bisrat Yared, a dedicated software engineering student, as a showcase of technical skills and love for Japanese pop culture.

Our Vision

AniStream was designed to provide anime enthusiasts and manga readers with a seamless and enjoyable experience. Whether you're diving into epic adventures, emotional dramas, or comedic stories, our platform is crafted to bring your favorite series to life, all in one place.

Built with Passion

As a software engineering student, I wanted to create a project that combines two of my greatest interests— technology and anime. AniStream is the result of countless hours spent coding, designing, and optimizing a platform that demonstrates both front-end and back-end development skills. From responsive design to user-friendly navigation, this site was built with modern tools and technologies such as Angular, Tailwind CSS, and more.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Anime Streaming: Watch your favorite anime shows in high quality.
  • Manga Reading: Dive into immersive manga stories with a user-friendly interface.
  • Custom Features: Tailored for an optimized viewing experience, including dynamic layouts, smooth transitions, and personalized genre recommendations.

Creating AniStream has been an exciting challenge that pushed me to apply everything I've learned as a student. Through this project, I aim to highlight my capabilities in software engineering, from handling asynchronous data to managing complex layouts and interactions.
